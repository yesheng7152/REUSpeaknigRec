'''Edward Lawrence Winn, Jr.''' (born August 22, 1919) is a [[United States|U.S.]] [[Republican Party (United States)|Republican]] [[politician]].

He was born in [[Kansas City, Missouri]].  He was elected as a Republican to the [[United States House of Representatives]] from [[Kansas]] and served from January 3, 1967 to January 3, 1985.  He currently resides in [[Prairie Village, Kansas]].
