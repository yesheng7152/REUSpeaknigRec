Walter Arthur Ronald Wiebe (b. September 28, 1912 in Rosthern, Saskatchewan - d. June 6, 1971 in Edmonton, Alberta) was a professional ice hockey player who played 414 games in the National Hockey League. He won the Stanley Cup in 1938. He played his entire career with the Chicago Black Hawks.
