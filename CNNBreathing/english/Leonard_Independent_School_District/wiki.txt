'''Leonard Independent School District''' is a public [[school district]] based in [[Leonard, Texas|Leonard]], [[Texas]] ([[United States|USA]]).

In 2009, the school district was rated "[[Texas Education Agency accountability ratings system|recognized]]" by the [[Texas Education Agency]].<ref>{{cite web|url = http://ritter.tea.state.tx.us/perfreport/account/2009/index.html|title = 2009 Accountability Rating System|publisher = Texas Education Agency}}</ref> The school's mascot is the Tiger and the colors are Blue and Gold. Their mascot's name is Paws.
