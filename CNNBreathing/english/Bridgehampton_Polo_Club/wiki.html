Bridgehampton Polo Club is a polo club in Bridgehampton, New York founded in the 1990s.[1] It is most widely known as the host club for the Mercedes Benz Polo Challenge at Two Trees Farm,[2] generally held in July of each year.

The Club is open for six consecutive Saturdays each year between July and August for public viewing.[3]

The Club colours are navy and white.[4]
