'''Bridgehampton Polo Club''' is a [[polo]] club in [[Bridgehampton, New York]] founded in the 1990s.<ref>[http://nymag.com/nymetro/travel/hamptons/features/6305/ New York Magazine]</ref> It is most widely known as the host club for the ''[[Mercedes Benz]] Polo Challenge'' at Two Trees Farm,<ref>[http://www.sportpolo.com/Spectators/bhpolo.htm Mercedes Benz Polo Challenge]</ref> generally held in July of each year.

The Club is open for six consecutive Saturdays each year between July and August for public viewing.<ref>[http://search.cityguide.aol.com/longisland/entertainment/bridgehampton-polo-club/v-121148 City Guide Long Island]</ref>

The Club colours are navy and white.<ref>[http://www.us-polo.org/clubs/bridgehampton.htm U.S. Polo Association]</ref>
